About Us

Rooted in Kilifi, reaching for the stars — nurturing confident, compassionate, and capable young leaders since our founding.

Our Purpose

A vibrant community of students, parents, and educators united in the pursuit of excellence and the creation of truly transformative learning experiences.

“We exist so every child in our care is seen, challenged, supported, and inspired to rise — becoming not just excellent students, but exceptional human beings.”

Our Story

Kilifi Rising Star Academy was founded with a clear conviction: coastal Kenya deserves a world-class yet deeply community-connected school where academic ambition meets warmth, creativity, and cultural pride.

Strategically located in the serene Mnarani area along Mnarani Boatyard Road / Mnarani Mjajani Road, we benefit from Kilifi’s natural beauty and rich heritage while offering modern facilities and forward-looking education aligned with Kenya’s Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC).

From our very first classroom to today’s growing campus, our focus remains unchanged: building strong foundations in primary, seamless progression through secondary, and preparing every student not only for examinations, but for life.

Our Leadership & Team

Behind every smiling student is a passionate, highly-trained educator. Our leadership team—headed by an experienced principal and supported by department heads, guidance counselors, and support staff—brings decades of combined expertise in CBC implementation, child development, and holistic education.

We invest continuously in professional growth so our teachers remain at the forefront of best practices while staying deeply connected to the Kilifi community they serve.
