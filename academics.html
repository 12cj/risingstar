Academics at Kilifi Rising Star Academy

Building strong foundations today for confident, capable leaders tomorrow — rooted in excellence, discipline, and transformative learning.

Primary Education (Grades 1–6)

Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC)

We proudly implement Kenya's Competency-Based Curriculum, focusing on practical skills, critical thinking, and real-world application rather than rote learning.

  • Strong emphasis on literacy, numeracy, and foundational life skills
  • Integrated learning areas: English, Kiswahili, Mathematics, Environmental Activities, Creative Arts, Physical & Health Education
  • Project-based assessments and learner-centered activities
  • Small class sizes for personalized attention and support
  • Christian values and moral education woven throughout

Secondary Education (Forms 1–4)

Comprehensive Preparation for KCSE & Beyond

Our secondary program bridges primary foundations to national examinations (KCSE) while nurturing leadership, independence, and future-ready skills.

  • Core subjects: English, Kiswahili, Mathematics, Biology, Chemistry, Physics, History & Government, Geography, CRE/IRE
  • Electives including Business Studies, Agriculture, Computer Studies (where available)
  • Targeted exam preparation with past papers, revision sessions, and mock exams
  • Holistic development: leadership training, career guidance, and mentorship
  • Integration of technology, research projects, and community service

Holistic Academic Approach

At Kilifi Rising Star Academy, academics go hand-in-hand with character building. We foster curiosity, resilience, discipline, and a love for learning in a supportive coastal environment.
