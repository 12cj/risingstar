Welcome to Kilifi Rising Star Academy

At Kilifi Rising Star Academy, we are more than a school — we are a thriving family united by a shared passion for excellence. Located along Mnarani Boatyard Road / Mnarani Mjajani Road in the beautiful coastal town of Kilifi, our premier primary and secondary institution nurtures every child's potential in a supportive, inspiring environment.

Our core mission is clear: to build a vibrant community of students, parents, and staff who strive for academic excellence while creating meaningful, transformative learning experiences that prepare young minds for a bright future.

We blend the strength of Kenya's Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) with enriched programs in arts, sports, technology, and life skills — all set against the breathtaking backdrop of Kilifi's beaches and culture.

Here, every child is seen, valued, and empowered to rise.

Seamless Excellence from Primary to Secondary

Primary (Grades 1–6)

We lay a strong foundation through engaging, play-based learning that builds confidence, creativity, and core competencies. Small class sizes ensure personalized attention.

Secondary (Forms 1–4)

Our secondary program prepares students for KCSE success while developing leadership, critical thinking, and real-world skills through advanced academics, career guidance, and extracurricular depth.

A Continuous Journey

Enjoy smooth progression from primary to secondary within the same nurturing community — no disruption, just continued growth and stronger relationships.
